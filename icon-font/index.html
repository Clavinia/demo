Our Mission

We're not always able to get the perfect home at the first try

This café is a refuge for lost souls, both human and cat. We aim to build a nice and comfy space where new friendships can grow, no matter the species. All the profits from this establishment are entierly dedicated to care and offer a safety beacon for our rescue cats. We want them to be healthy and loved, and we know we can give some of them the chance to find the perfect family or, at least, receive plenty of love and attention from our visitors.

We also aim to give you the chance to share time with a pet, if you are unable to. Nowadays adopting means having stability and sometimes we are in a place only for a while. That doesn't mean you can't have cat love! Come anytime to be showered with purrs and furry warmth in your lap. They can save you too!
